Data Quality Assessment Summary

1. All 10 datasets have been loaded and explored successfully.

2. Date variables such as `date`, `launch_date`, `month`, `transaction_date`, and `portfolio_date` are being recorded as strings and need to be transformed to date in the preprocessing stage.

3. There is a missing value in the `yoy_growth_pct` variable in the `04_monthly_sip_inflows.csv` dataset.

4. Numeric variables like NAV, AUM, returns, and expense ratios are being stored in numeric data types.

5. The validation for AMFI scheme codes showed that all 40 AMFI scheme codes from `01_fund_master.csv` can be found in the `02_nav_history.csv` dataset.

6. Fund Master dataset:

   - 10 unique fund houses
   - 2 categories (Equity and Debt)
   - 12 sub-categories
   - 5 risk categories

7. No major data issues were detected during initial exploration.
